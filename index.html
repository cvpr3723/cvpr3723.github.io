High resolution images

100 mega pixel UAV images of real agricultural fields.

Dense Crop and Weed annotations

Semantics for crops (sugar beet) and weeds.

Plant instances

Accurate plant instance annotations of crops and weeds.

Leaf instances

Leaf-level annotations of crop leaves.

Plant and Leaf Annotations

Dense pixel-wise annotations of over 5k plants including crops and weeds. Highly detailed, pixel-wise annotations of over 30k crop leaves.

Different growth stages

We captured data at three different days resulting in different growth stages and lighting conditions.

Benchmarks

Benchmarks for semantic segmentation, panoptic segmentation, detection, leaf instance segmentation, and hierarchical panoptic segmentation.
